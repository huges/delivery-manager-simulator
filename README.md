# The Delivery Manager Simulator

**What Would Ugo Say?**

A probabilistic meeting wisdom engine trained on 14 years of organizational data.

Generate realistic responses a delivery manager might produce under uncertain technical, organizational or metaphysical conditions.

---

## Features

- Random meeting quote generator  
- Rare Bagnasco Events™  
- Reality anomaly detection  
- Miracle-level responses (extremely improbable)  
- Legacy-powered decision support

---

## Example outputs

> Non si può fare.

> Mi sembra una soluzione coraggiosa. Ma non vedo eroi qui.

> ⚠ Reality anomaly detected  
> Per me possiamo andare in produzione rispettando i tempi.

---

## Probability Model

- 97% Normal operational pessimism  
- 2% Rare easter egg events  
- 1% Reality anomalies
